Things to do

Hanoi is the captiol city of Vietnam. Being such a important city it draws citizens from all over Vietnam to come and find work. It is a very crowded place and creates a very lively enviorment. One of the most enjoyable things I would like to do is to grab a seat at a BBQ joint at night when the tempertures are cooler and just enjy the vibe while grilling my own food. However just a two hour drive outside of the city you can find relaxation and scenery in Halong Bay. It has quite the unique gerography with skinny but tall mountians coming out of the water.
